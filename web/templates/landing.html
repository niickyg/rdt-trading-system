Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about how RDT Trading works.

What strategies does the system use?
RDT runs five independent strategies: RRS Momentum, RSI(2) Mean Reversion, Trend Breakout, Post-Earnings Drift, and Gap Fill. Each has its own capital allocation, risk limits, and ML model that adapts to market conditions.
How does the ML scoring work?
Each strategy has a dedicated GradientBoosting model trained on historical data with walk-forward validation. Models score signals 0-100% confidence based on technical features, market regime, and volatility conditions. Only signals above the confidence threshold are executed.
Do I need an Interactive Brokers account?
For live or paper trading execution, yes. IBKR is the sole data source and execution broker. You can start with paper trading to validate the system before committing real capital.
Can I customize risk settings?
Absolutely. You can adjust per-strategy capital allocation, maximum positions, risk per trade, daily loss limits, and maximum drawdown. The dashboard settings page gives you full control over all risk parameters.
