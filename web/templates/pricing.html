Frequently Asked Questions

What is Real Relative Strength (RRS)?

RRS measures how a stock is performing relative to SPY (the overall market), normalized by volatility. Stocks with high RRS are outperforming the market and showing momentum. Our threshold of 1.75+ identifies strong candidates.
