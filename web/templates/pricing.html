Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?
All features of your selected plan for 14 days. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to signals, the dashboard, and all tools included in your tier.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and upgrades are prorated.
Do you guarantee profits?
No. Trading involves risk and past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Our strategies are backtested and ML-optimized, but market conditions change and individual results will vary.
Do I need an Interactive Brokers account?
For automated execution, yes. You can also use the signals manually with any broker. The system provides entry, stop, and target prices for each signal.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards through Stripe. Annual plans can also be paid via wire transfer for enterprise customers.
