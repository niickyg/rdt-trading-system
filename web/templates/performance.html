Methodology

Walk-Forward Validation

Models are trained on rolling windows and validated on out-of-sample data. No lookahead bias. Parameters are re-optimized monthly using only data available at the time.

Capital Allocation

Starting capital: $25,000. Each strategy receives an independent allocation (10-40%). Position sizing is ATR-based with a maximum of 2% risk per trade. No compounding in backtests.

Live Start Date

Paper trading began February 2026. All live metrics are tracked from this date forward. Backtest period covers 2023-2025 daily data sourced from IBKR historical bars.

Data Source

All market data is sourced exclusively from Interactive Brokers. Daily OHLCV bars are cached locally and refreshed every 10 minutes during market hours.
